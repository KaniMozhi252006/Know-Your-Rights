Fundamental rights include Right to Equality, Right to Freedom, Right against Exploitation, Right to Freedom of Religion, Cultural and Educational Rights, and Right to Constitutional Remedies.

Pradhan Mantri Jan Dhan Yojana is a financial inclusion program aimed at expanding affordable access to financial services such as bank accounts, remittances, credit, insurance, and pensions.

The Right to Education Act ensures free and compulsory education for all children between the ages of 6 and 14 in India, providing equal opportunities for quality education.

Atal Pension Yojana is a government-backed pension scheme aimed at providing income security to workers in the unorganized sector by ensuring a fixed pension after retirement.

The Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana can be applied online through the official PMAY website. It provides financial assistance for purchasing or constructing houses for low-income groups.

The Pradhan Mantri Ujjwala Yojana aims to provide free LPG connections to women from Below Poverty Line (BPL) households.

Make in India is a national campaign aimed at encouraging domestic and foreign companies to manufacture products in India and boost the manufacturing sector.

Digital India aims to enhance online infrastructure and increase internet connectivity to make the country digitally empowered in the field of technology.

Passport Seva Yojana provides an easy online process to apply for a passport. You can register, pay fees, and schedule appointments on the official website.

Swachh Bharat Abhiyan is a national cleanliness campaign launched to eliminate open defecation, promote cleanliness, and ensure proper waste management across India.
